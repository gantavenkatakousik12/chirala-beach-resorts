## What Makes Andhra Coastal Food Special?

### The Heat
Andhra cuisine is famous for its liberal use of:
- **Guntur mirchi** ‚Äî India's hottest mainstream chili
- **Red chili powder** ‚Äî in quantities that would alarm other cuisines
- **Green chilies** ‚Äî for fresh, sharp heat

But it's not just about heat ‚Äî it's about **flavor depth** that happens to come with fire.

### The Freshness
In Chirala and Ramapuram Beach area:
- Fish is caught daily
- Prawns come straight from the boats
- Crab is still feisty when it reaches the kitchen
- No frozen imports ‚Äî everything is local

### The Technique
Generations of **coastal Andhra** cooks have perfected:
- Deep frying without making it greasy
- Curry-making that infuses flavor into every fiber
- Pickling techniques that preserve and enhance
- Rice preparations that complement every dish

## Spice Levels: A Guide for Non-Andhra Visitors

### Understanding the Heat:

| Level | Description | What to Expect |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| Mild | Still has kick | Pleasant warmth |
| Medium | Standard Andhra | Serious heat, manageable |
| Spicy | Local preference | You will sweat |
| Extra Spicy | For experts only | Requires training |

### How to Order:
- **"Mild ga cheyandi"** ‚Äî Make it mild (Telugu)
- Just say "less spicy please" ‚Äî staff understands
- Ask for curd/buttermilk on the side ‚Äî natural coolant

### Cooling Strategies:
1. **Curd rice** ‚Äî The ultimate Andhra cooler
2. **Buttermilk (Majjiga)** ‚Äî Traditional coolant
3. **Jaggery/Sugar** ‚Äî Works better than water
4. **Banana** ‚Äî Coats the mouth
5. **Don't drink water immediately** ‚Äî It spreads the heat!
