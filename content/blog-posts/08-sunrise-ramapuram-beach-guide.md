## Photography Guide: Capturing the Perfect Sunrise

### Equipment Recommendations:

**Essential:**
- Any camera (even smartphones work!)
- Tripod (for low-light shots)
- Clean lens/screen

**Recommended:**
- Wide-angle lens (to capture sky drama)
- ND filter (for long exposures)
- Remote shutter release

### Camera Settings:

**For DSLR/Mirrorless:**
- Mode: Manual or Aperture Priority
- Aperture: f/8 to f/11 (for sharpness)
- ISO: Start at 100, increase as needed
- Shutter: 1/125 to 1/500 (depending on light)
- White Balance: Cloudy or Shade (for warmer tones)

**For Smartphones:**
- Enable HDR mode
- Use manual/pro mode if available
- Lower ISO
- Tap on the brightest part to expose for sky
- Use gridlines for composition

### Composition Tips:

1. **Include foreground** â€” shells, footprints, boats
2. **Use rule of thirds** â€” horizon on lower third
3. **Shoot both portrait and landscape**
4. **Look behind you** â€” sometimes the western sky reflects beautifully
5. **Capture silhouettes** â€” palm trees, birds, people
