### 2. Vodarevu Beach (The Peaceful One)

**Location**: 15 km from Chirala town
**Crowd Level**: Extremely Low
**Best For**: Solitude seekers, photography, meditation

Vodarevu is where locals go when they want complete peace:

- Even more secluded than Ramapuram
- Traditional fishing village atmosphere
- Morning fish markets
- Spectacular photography opportunities
- Almost no commercial development

**Pro Tip**: Combine Ramapuram stay with a day trip to Vodarevu for the complete experience.

### 3. Chirala Town Beach (The Convenient One)

**Location**: Chirala town itself
**Crowd Level**: Moderate (local visitors)
**Best For**: Quick visits, sunset watching

The beach near Chirala town is more accessible but less pristine:

- Good for evening walks
- Local food stalls nearby
- More crowds on weekends
- Easy access from town

## Local Secrets Only Insiders Know

### 1. The Best Sunrise Spot
At Ramapuram Beach, walk 500 meters north from Rainbow Beach Resorts for unobstructed sunrise views without any structures in frame.

### 2. Fresh Fish Markets
Vodarevu fishing village has morning catches around 6-7 AM. Buy fresh fish and many resorts will cook it for you!

### 3. Handloom Deals
Chirala is famous for handloom. Visit the weaving communities in Ipurupalem for factory-direct prices.

### 4. Full Moon Nights
The beach during full moon is magical. The silver light on the waves is unforgettable.

### 5. Turtle Nesting Season
Between December and March, Olive Ridley turtles nest on these beaches. Early mornings might reward you with sightings.
