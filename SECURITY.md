# Security Policy for Rainbow Beach Resorts

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this repository, please email us immediately at:

**security@rainbowbeachresorts.in**

Or contact our team at:
- **Phone:** +91 7799111273 / +91 7799111274
- **Primary Email:** rainbowbeachresorts@gmail.com

**Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had a chance to address it.**

## What to Include

When reporting a security vulnerability, please include:
1. Description of the vulnerability
2. Affected components or areas
3. Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
4. Potential impact
5. Your contact information

## Response Timeline

- **Initial Response:** Within 24 hours
- **Investigation:** Within 3-5 business days
- **Fix & Release:** As soon as a patch is available
- **Public Disclosure:** After a reasonable grace period (typically 30 days)

## Scope

This policy covers security vulnerabilities in:
- The Rainbow Beach Resorts website
- The GitHub repository
- Web applications and services
- Server infrastructure (when applicable)

## Out of Scope

The following are generally out of scope:
- Spam or social engineering
- Missing HTTP security headers (unless critical)
- Outdated dependencies (without demonstrable impact)
- Theoretical vulnerabilities without proof of concept

## Responsible Disclosure

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will:
- Acknowledge receipt of your report
- Provide updates on our progress
- Credit you as the discoverer (if desired)
- Work collaboratively to resolve the issue

Thank you for helping keep Rainbow Beach Resorts secure!
